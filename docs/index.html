Abstract

HoliBench (Holistic Benchmark) is a flexible benchmarking framework designed to evaluate foundation models, including LLMs, VLMs, and time-series models, across diverse hardware platforms ranging from servers to embedded devices. Addressing the need for consistent performance profiling, HoliBench offers automatic device detection and real-time monitoring of key metrics such as latency, accuracy, and energy consumption. Our approach enables rapid swapping of models and scenarios with minimal configuration, ensuring easy extensibility and cross-platform compatibility. The results demonstrate HoliBench's capability to provide comprehensive insights into model performance, significantly simplifying the benchmarking process for researchers and developers.
